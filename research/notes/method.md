# Method

Status: provisional. This method governs future work but does not verify any
source or claim.

## Evidence Levels

The project separates source text, observation, metaphor or symbolic-language
classification, biblical quotation, biblical allusion, religious typology,
providential claim, role construction, interpretation, broader claim,
alternative reading, and conclusion.

## Source Handling

Raw files are immutable. Normalized text is separate. Every acquired source
must record repository, URL, retrieval date, hash, rights status, acquisition
method, verification status, and attribution caveat where applicable.

## Passage Handling

Every coded passage needs a stable source ID, locator, date, corpus period,
document type, audience or recipient where known, public/private/report status,
source tier, and faithful text or paraphrase.

## Interpretation Rules

- Do not collapse all religious language into metaphor.
- Do not treat metaphor frequency as interpretive importance by itself.
- Do not classify death as sacrifice without Brown's language or an explicit
  interpretive warrant.
- Do not use later reception as evidence of Brown's self-representation.
- Preserve textual variants and conflicting reports.
- Mark uncertain allusions as uncertain, especially when thesis-bearing.

## Claim Control

No hypothesis becomes a claim without source-grounded evidence, alternative
reading, confidence level, and review status. Claims about private belief
require explicit evidence and human approval.
