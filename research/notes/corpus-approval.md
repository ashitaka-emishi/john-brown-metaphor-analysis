# Corpus Approval

Date recorded: 2026-07-16

Approving scholar: Andrew Hammer

## Approval

The scholar approves proceeding with Goal 10 corpus acquisition using
`research/data/source-register.csv` and `research/notes/source-acquisition-plan.md`
as the acquisition queue and scope.

Approved work is limited to legally accessible public-domain or openly available
source texts and metadata. Copyrighted secondary works listed in the source
register are approved only for lawful access notes and bibliographic metadata;
they are not approved for bulk local reproduction.

## Conditions

- Prefer authoritative repositories, documented downloads, APIs, or stable
  public pages.
- Preserve raw content and normalized text separately.
- Record provenance, retrieval method, access date, rights status, and SHA-256
  hashes for every acquired source.
- Preserve Gettysburg Address manuscript witnesses or authoritative
  representations as separate sources; do not silently harmonize variants.
- Split grouped or unresolved source-register rows when acquisition identifies
  distinct sources.
- Stop if a required source cannot be legally or reliably acquired, provenance
  is uncertain, access controls block acquisition, or variants remain unresolved.

## Notes

This approval authorizes corpus acquisition and normalization. It does not
approve interpretive claims, final corpus boundaries beyond the documented
queue, publication, or use of unverified quotations as evidence.
