# Codebook Approval

Date: 2026-07-21

Status: approved for acquisition-stage use with limits.

The scholar approved the draft codebook as part of the corpus-approval gate.
This approval supports source acquisition and later preparation for coding, but
it does not approve any classification, interpretation, thesis, finding, or
publication.

Future coding must still stop when:

- a metaphor classification depends only on analyst intuition;
- a biblical allusion is uncertain and thesis-bearing;
- typology is inferred from pious vocabulary alone;
- death is equated with sacrifice without Brown's language or a clearly
  identified interpretive warrant;
- Brown's self-representation is conflated with later reception.
