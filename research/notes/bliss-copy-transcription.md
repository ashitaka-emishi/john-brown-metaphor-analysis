## Status

The raw PDF is a valid manuscript-image representation of the Bliss / White
House Copy. `pdftotext` produced no usable text, and OCR failed verification
with severe recognition errors. The normalized file is therefore a human
transcription by Andrew Hammer from the manuscript images.

The transcription has been approved to unblock Goal 10 acquisition. For
quotation-level evidence, check the normalized text against the manuscript
image and the transcription notes in the normalized file.
