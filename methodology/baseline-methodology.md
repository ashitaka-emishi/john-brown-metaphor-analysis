# Baseline Methodology

Status: migration baseline for the John Brown project.

The repository preserves a governed scholarly build-system method:

- human scholarly authority over question, corpus, interpretation, thesis, and
  publication;
- source-grounded evidence with raw and normalized files separated;
- explicit schemas for sources, evidence, claims, process events, and
  interventions;
- stable IDs for sources, observations, claims, reviews, and methodology
  events;
- separation of source text, observation, classification, interpretation,
  claim, and conclusion;
- disconfirming evidence and alternative readings;
- documented human gates;
- validation and tests before completion claims;
- publication only after human approval.

This baseline is methodological architecture, not evidence that the method is
validated for all scholarly projects.
