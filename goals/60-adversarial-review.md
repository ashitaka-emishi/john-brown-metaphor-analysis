# Goal 60 - Adversarial Review

## Objective

Attempt to disprove, narrow, or materially improve the draft before publication
review.

## Required Inputs

- Draft paper
- Evidence matrix
- Claims table
- Prior reviews and audits

## Required Artifacts

- Historical review
- Rhetorical review
- Theological-language review
- Source and citation review
- Critical countervoice review
- Anachronism review
- Disconfirmation review
- Coding review

## Completion Criteria

- Each critique is accepted, rejected, or partially accepted with reasons.
- Successful critiques revise the evidence, claims, or draft.
- No critical citation or quotation failures remain.
- `python -m john_brown_research validate` and `pytest` pass.

## Stop Conditions

Stop if review finds unresolved source, attribution, quotation, access,
scope, or interpretation failures.
