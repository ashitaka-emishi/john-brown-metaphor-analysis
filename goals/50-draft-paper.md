# Goal 50 - Draft the Paper

## Objective

Draft the scholarly paper from approved thesis claims and source-grounded
evidence.

## Required Inputs

- Thesis approval
- Claims table
- Evidence matrix
- Paper scaffold
- Bibliography

## Required Artifacts

- `paper/paper.qmd`
- `paper/references.bib`
- Draft source audit

## Completion Criteria

- Material quotations are verified.
- The paper follows the approved scope and thesis.
- Counterevidence and limitations are represented.
- Brown's self-representation is not conflated with reception.
- `python -m john_brown_research validate` and `pytest` pass.

## Stop Conditions

Stop if quotation, attribution, citation, scope, or claim-mapping failures
remain unresolved.
