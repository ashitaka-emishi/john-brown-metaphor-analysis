# Goal 40 - Diachronic Analysis and Thesis

## Objective

Analyze change and continuity across corpus periods and produce a provisional
thesis grounded in evidence and counterevidence.

## Required Inputs

- Evidence matrix
- Coding memo
- Dossier claim inventory
- Disconfirming tests

## Required Artifacts

- Claims table
- Argument map
- Disconfirmation review
- Provisional thesis
- Findings note

## Completion Criteria

- Every thesis clause maps to supporting and counter evidence.
- Claims distinguish Brown's wording from reports and later recollections.
- Claims distinguish Brown's self-representation from later reception.
- Claims about private belief are avoided unless explicitly approved.
- `python -m john_brown_research validate` and `pytest` pass.

## Stop Conditions

Stop at the thesis-approval human gate. Also stop if evidence materially
contradicts the working thesis or if a required period lacks adequate evidence.
