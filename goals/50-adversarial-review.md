# Goal 50 — Conduct Adversarial Scholarly Review

## Objective

Attempt to disprove, narrow, or materially improve the draft.


## Operating contract

Read `AGENTS.md`, the `$scholarly-research` skill, and the files named below.
Work in checkpoints. Update `logs/progress.md`.
Do not proceed past a human gate.
Run `python -m lincoln_research validate` and `pytest` before completion.


## Required reports

- `research/reviews/historical-review.md`
- `research/reviews/rhetorical-review.md`
- `research/reviews/theological-language-review.md`
- `research/reviews/source-and-citation-review.md`
- `research/reviews/critical-countervoice-review.md`
- `research/reviews/anachronism-review.md`

## Required attacks

- Ceremonial convention is mistaken for theory.
- Sacrifice is imposed rather than demonstrated.
- Rhetoric is mistaken for private belief.
- The Civil War is generalized into war as such.
- Soldier agency is ignored.
- Omission of bodily violence is called intentional concealment without evidence.
- The Second Inaugural's judgment frame is understated.
- Lincoln is not adequately distinguished from Everett.

Revise the paper and claims when critiques succeed.

## Completion criteria

- Each critique is accepted, rejected, or partially accepted with evidence.
- No critical citation failures remain.
- Conclusion reflects narrowed claims.
- Validation and tests pass.
