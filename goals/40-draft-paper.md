# Goal 40 — Draft the Scholarly Paper

## Objective

Draft a 7,000–9,000 word paper traceable to approved evidence and thesis.


## Operating contract

Read `AGENTS.md`, the `$scholarly-research` skill, and the files named below.
Work in checkpoints. Update `logs/progress.md`.
Do not proceed past a human gate.
Run `python -m lincoln_research validate` and `pytest` before completion.


## Preconditions

Human approval is recorded in `research/notes/thesis-approval.md`.

## Required sections

1. Introduction and methodological limits
2. Historical and ceremonial context
3. War as the test of an embodied proposition
4. Soldier death and national life
5. Consecration and interpretive authority
6. The dead as a claim upon the living
7. New birth, emancipation, and transformation
8. Providence, judgment, and counterevidence
9. Conclusion

## Requirements

- Draft in `paper/paper.qmd`.
- Cite through `paper/references.bib`.
- Include a methods statement.
- Define sacrifice.
- Distinguish observation, interpretation, and inference.
- Address Everett and reception.
- Include the strongest alternative reading.
- Create `research/reviews/draft-source-audit.md`.

## Completion criteria

- Material quotations are verified.
- Material historical claims are cited.
- Draft stays within scope.
- Source audit has no unresolved critical failures.
- Validation and tests pass.
